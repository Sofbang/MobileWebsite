Mobility that Matters

Developed by Oracle Platinum Partner, Sofbang, Oracle Public Sector Mobile Showcase Applications demonstrate the power of the Oracle Mobile Application Framework (MAF) development environment. More importantly, they show the value of utilizing mobility in government, healthcare, and higher education enterprises. All of the showcase applications include integrated Oracle mobile messaging and high level integration story arcs.

Citizen Engagement:
Metro 311

Citizens can take an active role in the care of their community by quickly logging and tracking service requests. Metro 311 also puts citizen service information in the palm of their hand.

  • iOS
  • Android

Streamlined Applications:
Metro Schools

Reduce paper processing with mobile application submissions. Metro Schools mobile application allows guardians to enroll their students right from their mobile phone. They can receive reminder notifications, access immunization records, and attach required documents without a trip to the post office.

  • iOS
  • Android

Workforce Productivity:
Case Management

Enable your mobile workforce to perform on the go. The Child Welfare mobile application demonstrates the power of productivity with the right information at the right time.
