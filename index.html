Developed by Oracle Platinum Partner, Sofbang, Oracle Public Sector Mobile Showcase Applications demonstrate the power of the Oracle Mobile Application Framework (MAF) development environment. More importantly, they show the value of utilizing mobility in government, healthcare, and higher education enterprises. All of the showcase applications include integrated Oracle mobile messaging and high level integration story arcs.

Citizen Engagement:
Metro 311

Citizens can take an active role in the care of their community by quickly logging and tracking service requests. Metro 311 also puts citizen service information in the palm of their hand.

  • Android

Streamlined Applications:
Metro Schools

Reduce paper processing with mobile application submissions. Metro Schools mobile application allows guardians to enroll their students right from their mobile phone. They can receive reminder notifications, access immunization records, and attach required documents without a trip to the post office.

  • Android

Workforce Productivity:
Case Management

Sofbang’s Mobile Consulting Offerings

Simplify your end-user experience, extend your backend applications and discover new opportunities for engagement, growth and productivity with mobile.

Sofbang’s Mobile Consulting offerings include:

Mobile Needs Assessment (MNA)

Our Mobile Needs Assessment assists customers in assessing the current mobile state of the enterprise. We define the vision and desired target state, identify gaps and establish a realizable roadmap for an enterprise mobile strategy.

Enterprise Mobile Solutions

Sofbang’s Enterprise Mobile Solutions allow our customers to securely integrate their backend applications, streamline business processes and provide access to data no matter where they are or what devices they use.

Oracle Mobility

As an Oracle Platinum Partner, we deliver mobility solutions that leverage Oracle Fusion Middleware and the Oracle Cloud – specifically Oracle Mobile Application Framework (Oracle MAF), Oracle Mobile Suite (OMS) and Oracle Mobile Security Suite (OMSS).

Bespoke Mobile Solutions

Our bespoke mobile solutions allow us to create apps leveraging Oracle Mobile solutions. We work with our customers to identify and assess their mobile needs and conceive, design and build apps tailored to their precise business purpose, with secure backend integrations that are easy to use and easy to update.
